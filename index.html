My Story

As a personal stylist, my goal is to empower my clients through smart, sustainable style choices. I help both men and women understand the basics of great style, and how to achieve dress-and-go confidence every day.

I've worked in many areas of the fashion industry, from design to merchandising to online media, which has allowed me to gain an insider's perspective. From a personal standpoint, I know firsthand the effects of wardrobe anxiety and image-related frustrations. Pulling from both experiences, I'm able to offer my clients a sincere, professional, and lighthearted approach to mastering great style.

Since its launch in 2011, my business (formerly named The Re-Stylist) has served clients in both New York and San Francisco, and has been featured on several media outlets including eHow.com and Dr. Oz's YouBeauty. As of 2013, I now work with clients from all over the globe with my signature virtual styling program. I'd love to work with you!
